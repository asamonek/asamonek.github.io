<h4>Innovation for Independence - Social and Behavioral Change Communications Strategy for Refugees in Poland (UNHCR, January-August 2025)</h4>
<details>
  	<summary>Read more</summary>
  	<br>This Social and Behavior Change Communication (SBCC) strategy has been developed as part of a UNHCR initiative to support Ukrainian refugees and residents of collective shelters, as well as women from female-headed households in Poland, in taking steps toward greater independence and inclusion.<br><br>
  		The strategy provides a roadmap for designing and implementing communications activities that promote three priority behaviors:
  	<ul>
  		<li>Seeking and maintaining formal employment</li>
  		<li>Pursuing independent and stable living arrangements</li>
  		<li>Actively engaging in language learning.</li>
  	</ul>
  	This document is intended to guide stakeholders in developing targeted, insight-driven communication activities. It outlines the behavioral rationale, strategic approach, and the design process for interventions.<br><br>
  	Read the project report at the <a href="https://data.unhcr.org/en/documents/details/118177">UNHCR Operational Data Portal</a>.
</details>
